Auto-sync setup
Build an Apple Shortcut that pulls your Health data daily and copies it to clipboard. The app reads it on open.
What you build: An iOS Shortcut that runs every morning at 07:00, reads your last 24h of Health data, formats as JSON, copies to clipboard, and opens this app.
1Open Shortcuts app
On your iPhone open Shortcuts (built-in Apple app, gray icon). Tap + top right to create new Shortcut.
2Add Health actions
Search for "Find Health Samples". Add 4 of them — one for each metric:
• Heart Rate Variability — Last 24 hours
• Resting Heart Rate — Last 24 hours
• Sleep Analysis — Last 24 hours
• Weight — Most recent
3Build the JSON
Add a "Text" action. Type or paste the format below, replacing the placeholders with magic variables from step 2 (tap the small chip to insert): {"data":{"metrics":[ {"name":"heart_rate_variability","data":[{"date":"[Current Date]","qty":[HRV value]}]}, {"name":"resting_heart_rate","data":[{"date":"[Current Date]","qty":[RHR value]}]} ]}}
4Copy to clipboard
Add "Copy to Clipboard" action. Input: the Text from step 3.
5Open Recovery app
Add "Open URLs" action with URL: https://fidesbv.github.io/Recovery Name the Shortcut "Sync Recovery" and save.
6Schedule for 07:00 daily
In Shortcuts app, tap Automation tab → + → Time of Day → 07:00 → Daily.
Action: Run Shortcut "Sync Recovery".
Toggle "Run Immediately" ON so it runs without asking.
7Test it
Run the Shortcut manually once. The app should open and show a green banner "Health data ready". Tap to import.
💡 Why a tap? iOS Safari requires a tap to read clipboard (security). After Shortcut runs at 07:00, you open the app once and tap the green banner — takes 2 seconds.
Nutrition for lean + strength
Simple principles. No tracking. Apply as habit.
🥩 Protein — most important
Aim for ~1.8g per kg bodyweight daily (170g at 96 kg). Critical for muscle preservation during fat loss.

Rule of thumb per meal: palm-sized portion of meat/fish/eggs/cottage cheese × 4 meals = ~170g.
⚖️ Caloric deficit — for weight loss
Modest deficit: 400-500 kcal/day below maintenance = 0.5 kg/week loss.

Without counting: remove sugar, snacks, alcohol weeknights. Don't shrink portions of whole foods.
🥗 Meal structure
Each main meal:
• Protein (palm-sized) — meat, fish, eggs, cottage cheese, tofu
• Vegetables (2 handfuls) — varied colors
• Carbs (cupped hand) — rice, potato, oats — adjust to training
• Fats (thumb-sized) — olive oil, avocado, nuts
⏰ Timing
Protein spread over 4 meals — 40-45g each. Don't load everything at dinner.

Carbs around training (before or after). Less on rest days.
💧 Hydration
3-3.5L water daily. Add electrolytes after sweaty training.

Coffee morning OK. Stop caffeine after 14:00 — affects HRV and deep sleep.
🍷 Alcohol — your context
You stopped weekend whiskey early April — keep it that way. Each glass drops HRV by 5-10% for 24-48h and crushes deep sleep.

Occasional glass is OK not in last 3h before bed.
🎯 For YOUR goal (96 → 85 kg)
Realistic target: 0.4-0.5 kg per week = 11 kg in 5-6 months.

Single biggest lever: remove liquid calories — alcohol, juice, sugary drinks.

Second biggest: protein at every meal — keeps you full, preserves muscle.
